Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy béchamel, and melted cheese. Each bite is a perfect balance of savory flavors and comforting textures.

Often baked to golden perfection, lasagna is a hearty and satisfying meal that’s perfect for family dinners or special occasions. It’s a beloved favorite around the world for its rich taste and filling nature.

Steps to make the dish

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef with chopped onions and garlic until fully cooked.
  4. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and Italian herbs; simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese with egg, salt, and pepper.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta mixture, and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with mozzarella on top.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 30-40 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
