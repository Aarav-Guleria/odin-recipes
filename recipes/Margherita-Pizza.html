Margherita Pizza

Description

Margherita pizza is a traditional Italian dish featuring a simple yet delicious combination of fresh tomatoes, mozzarella cheese, and basil leaves. The ingredients are arranged on a thin crust and baked to perfection for a light, flavorful experience.

Known for its vibrant colors that reflect the Italian flag, Margherita pizza is a symbol of Italy’s rich culinary heritage. It's a favorite for those who appreciate fresh, high-quality ingredients and classic taste.

Steps to make the dish

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread a thin layer of tomato sauce over the dough.
  4. Add fresh slices of mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with fresh basil leaves and drizzle a bit of olive oil.
  6. Bake the pizza on a preheated pizza stone or baking tray for 10-12 minutes.
  7. Remove from oven when the crust is golden and cheese is bubbling.
  8. Let cool slightly, then slice and serve hot.
